About

We are about Bitcoin education and aim to:

  • Translate and share learning material (training courses, video lessons, articles, podcasts, blogs)
  • Bring together authors, translators and programmers to provide learning material accessible with web browsers and mobiles
  • Provide a platform to meet others to learn together

Get involved

Send your ideas and feedback, make a request, become a volunteer translator, check updates, become a mentor, join the community and help yourself and others gain financial freedom.

Email: bitcoin.training.courses@protonmail.com
