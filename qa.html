Questions and Answers

How can I help with translation?
  1. Visit the page with the original text you wish to translate
  2. Send an email including:
    1. The page URL (from the browser address window).
    2. The original text (Copy and Paste from the website).
    3. The translation of the text.
    4. Please indicate whether you wish to be mentioned on the About page as one of the contributing translators. You can include a personal profile page, such as a Twitter or Linkedin address to be included with your name.
    5. Email to: bitcoin.training.courses@protonmail.com
  3. Please note that all the content on this website has the Creative Commons CC BY license for free distribution.
  4. The translation is normally checked by a reviewer after which it will be published on the site.
How can I take a course?
  1. Use the website and start learning!
    (for working offline, download the course ZIP file).
  2. All the courses are free to use without any restriction.
  3. We encourage you to email questions. We try to reply directly and add frequently asked questions to the QA page.
  4. Please email (bitcoin.training.courses@protonmail.com) and tell us whether you want to be put in touch with others who are learning the same course in your language.
What should I do if I have questions and need help?
  1. Please do email (bitcoin.training.courses@protonmail.com) your questions, we will try to reply to all emails and answer your question directly.
  2. Your question may help others too because we will add the frequently asked question to the QA page.
  3. Please email and tell us whether you want to be put in touch with others who are learning the same course in your language. It is much more effective learning to partner with others.
  4. We can assist groups of learners with setting up social media groups.
  5. Teachers may be available for online lessons.
