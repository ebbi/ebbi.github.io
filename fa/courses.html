قبل از مطالعه بیت کوین، داشتن درک کامل از اصول اولیه پول، تاریخچه آن و سیستم مالی فعلی ضروری است. درک این مفاهیم پایه محکمی برای درک ماهیت انقلابی بیت کوین را فراهم می کند. با یادگیری در مورد تکامل پول، می‌توانید پتانسیل‌ها و محدودیت‌های سیستم مالی فعلی و چگونگی هدف بیت‌کوین برای مقابله با آنها را بهتر درک کنید. بدون این پایه، درک کامل اهمیت و تاثیر بالقوه بیت کوین می تواند چالش برانگیز باشد. به فرآیند یادگیری اعتماد کنید و متمرکز بمانید، زیرا پاداش درک عمیق و قدردانی از این رشته پیشرفته ارزش آن را دارد.

A message from "My First Bitcoin" founder

Bitcoin and Education

Bitcoin will change the world. That is certain. What we get to decide is how.

Bitcoin is much more than digital money. It can be a powerful force that will not only shape our future world, but our future selves. When used correctly, Bitcoin can be a tool that empowers the individual. And I know that sounds vague now, but by the end of this course you will have a basic framework of the why and the how.

The foundation of ensuring that we make the right choices will be education. The world is changing. The choice you have is whether you decide how it changes or you let someone else decide for you.

Table of Content

It is essential to have a firm grasp on the basics of money, its history, and the current financial system before studying Bitcoin. Understanding these concepts provides a strong foundation for comprehending the unique and disruptive nature of Bitcoin. By learning about the evolution of money, you will be able to better understand the potential and limitations of the current financial system and how Bitcoin aims to address them. Without this foundation, it may be challenging to fully appreciate the significance and potential impact of Bitcoin. Trust the process of learning and stay focused, as the reward of a deeper understanding and appreciation of this cutting-edge field will be well worth it.
